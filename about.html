About Rejoice Institute House

More than a publishing house, we are a home for writers and creatives.

Who Are We

Rejoice Institute House is a publishing institute designed to support writers, poets, and artists at every stage of their creative journey. We provide a platform for authors to publish, refine, and share their work with audiences far and wide.

Our Vision

We aim to be a bridge for writers to reach audiences without the stress of complicated contracts. We welcome new authors, those with large volumes of unpublished work, and anyone seeking guidance and community. Here, your incomplete ideas can become completed projects, and your creativity can thrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a space for freedom of expression in books, poetry, journals, letters, drawings, paintings, and other forms of creative art. We aspire to inspire, encourage, and nurture artists while creating a second home where creativity can flourish for decades.

What You Can Expect

Expect more than publishing services. We help refine your work, market it, and see it reach its potential. We support visual art, audio art, and other forms of creative expression, creating a marketplace for creators to showcase their talent.
