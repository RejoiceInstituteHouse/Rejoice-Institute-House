Welcome to Rejoice Institute House

Empowering writers, poets, and creatives to share their work, connect with audiences, and express themselves freely. Your story, your voice, your art—published without barriers.

Our Vision

At Rejoice Institute House, our vision is to be the bridge for new and established writers to reach their audience without navigating complex contracts or agreements. We provide a platform for authors without financial means to publish their work, and for those with abundant content who need a place to share it. We close the gap and stress of publishing by making it simple and accessible. More than a publishing house, we are an institute: a place to complete incomplete pieces, exchange ideas, and learn the craft of writing and publishing.

Our Mission

Our mission is to survive and thrive for decades, spreading the freedom of creative expression through books, poetry, journals, letters, drawings, paintings, and even love poetry. Rejoice Institute House is a second home for creatives—a place of art, encouragement, and inspiration. We want every writer to experience the gift of free expression and to share it with the world.

What You Can Expect

Visitors and contributors can expect more than a standard publishing house. We help refine books, market them, and see them reach their full potential. We provide clear, understandable, and creative content—both visual and audio—turning your work into a full-fledged creative experience. Think of this as a marketplace for creatives, where your art can flourish and reach its audience.
